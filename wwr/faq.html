FAQ

Do you provide any discounts on airfare?

Yes! We have multiple airlines that we partner with to provide our adventurers with discounted rates. Call or email using the contact number at the bottom of this page to ask for more information or a list of airlines.

Is it possible to reserve a private raft for one party?

This is absolutely possible under the right circumstances. For safety reasons, we cannot have too few or too many people per raft. If you would like to charter a private raft, your party must have no less than 6 individuals per raft. If there are less than this, you will be paired up with other adventurers.

Can I add an extra person to my party after we have already booked our trip?

Yes! Give us a call or send us an email using the contact information at the bottom of this page to add more adventurers to your party.

Is it possible to get a refund?

It is possible to receive a refund for guided camping trips for a prepaid trip if the trip is scheduled more than 1 month away from the date the refund was initially requested. Unfortunately, since space is limited, white water rafting trips are not refundable.

For any questions we have not addressed, feel free to contact us by phone at 1-800-999-9999 or by email at whitewaterrafting@gmail.com!
